About LLMP Logistics

LLMP (PTY) Ltd is the brainchild of Ms Palesa Maunda and Ms Lebohang Maunda and was conceptualized in early 2025, officially starting operations in March 2025. Our mission is to make a difference in people's lives and contribute positively to the economy of South Africa.

With great passion and experience in the transport sector, we bring a combination of skills and experience to offer clients quality service and great value for money.

Our vision is to be the favoured leader in the transport industry, providing excellent and timeous service to all clients where quality services and affordable logistics are key.

LLMP Logistics is a BBBEE level 1 company, 100% black owned, 100% black woman owned, and 100% black youth owned.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your logistics solutions cost-effective?

The company provides the highest quality, efficiently and professionally at a reasonable cost.

What does LLMP Logistics do?

The company provides multiple services which includes abnormal load escorts, abnormal load transport and other forms of transport including multi-axles, abnormal equipment, abnormal load permits, Plant hire services and 32ton flat decks.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Safety is our top priority. All our vehicles are regularly maintained and certified roadworthy. Our drivers are highly trained professionals with specialized certifications for abnormal load transport.

What is your BBBEE status?

LLMP Logistics is a BBBEE Level 1 contributor. We are 100% black-owned, 100% black woman-owned, and 100% black youth-owned, which makes us an ideal partner for organizations looking to strengthen their own BBBEE credentials through procurement.
